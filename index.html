Outreach

I began working as an Assistant Professor at Lehman College in Spring 2000. Before that, I completed postdocs at Johns Hopkins and Harvard after completing the doctoral program at the Courant Institute at NYU in 1996. I am also a doctoral faculty member at the CUNY Graduate Center and a visitor at Stony Brook, where I work with doctoral students and postdocs conducting mathematics research. In Fall of 2013 I was a visiting research professor at MSRI as part of the program on Mathematical General Relativity. In Fall 2018 to Spring 2019 I was on leave to serve as a Member of the Institute for Advanced Study (IAS) at Princeton for the program on Variational Methods in Geometry lead by Prof Fernando Coda Marques. Additionally, I was also a visitor at Simons Center for Geometry and Physics (SCGP). Here is a Report on my 2018-2019 Fellowship Leave (pdf). Finally, in 2013 I was nominated for (link)American Mathematical Society Fellow by Shing-Tung Yau and by Bill Minicozzi. See link for 2013 Research Description.
